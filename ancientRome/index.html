Landmarks & Engineering (Chronological Order)

1. Roman Road System

The foundation of Roman expansion and empire control.

2. Aqueducts

Engineering systems that delivered fresh water to cities.

3. Circus Maximus

Chariot racing stadium for massive public events.

4. The Colosseum

Iconic amphitheater used for gladiator games.

5. Arch of Titus

Triumphal arch celebrating Roman military success.

6. Column of Trajan

Stone column carved with Roman war victories.

7. The Pantheon

Famous dome showcasing Roman architectural skill.

8. Baths of Caracalla

Large public baths used for hygiene and social life.
